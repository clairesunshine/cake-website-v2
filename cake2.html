How to Make the Cake

Step 1

Gather your ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1.5 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 cups whole milk

Step 2

Preheat your oven to 325 degrees and grease your cake pan.

Step 3

First, mix the butter and sugar. Then, add the eggs and vanilla. Last, stir in the flour.

Step 4

Pour the batter into the pan. Then, stick it in the oven and bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

And that's it!
